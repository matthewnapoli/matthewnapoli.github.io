Approach

Soft structure. Real progress.

Sessions start by finding the exact point of confusion, then build toward independent practice. Students leave with a short recap, next steps, and a little more academic calm than they came in with.

01 Diagnose

Review homework, notes, tests, or goals to pinpoint the real barrier.

02 Explain

Use plain language, examples, and visuals until the concept clicks.

03 Practice

Work through fresh problems together, then hand control back to the student.
